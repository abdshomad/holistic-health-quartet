
# Holistic Health Quartet

A specialized health recovery blueprint generator for professionals facing complex metabolic and biomechanical challenges.

## The Experts
- **Dr. Budi Santoso (Internal Medicine/Metabolic)**: Focuses on managing the "Metabolic Storm" (Diabetes & Hypertension).
- **Dr. Rahmat Hidayat (Spine Surgeon)**: Protects structural integrity from vibration and static sitting trauma.
- **Dr. Retno Wulandari (Retina Consultant)**: Monitors the eyes as a window to vascular health.
- **Bung Ade Rai (Fitness Specialist)**: Bridges medical advice into actionable lifestyle armor through muscle training.

## Key Features
- **Integrated Analysis**: Avoids "silo thinking" by connecting metabolic issues with biomechanical pain and eye fatigue.
- **Dynamic Profile Audit**: Users can input specific routines, medical histories, and commute details for personalized advice.
- **Quadrangulation Strategy**: Synthesizes inputs into a coherent recovery path.
- **Iron-IT Lifestyle**: Practical strategies specifically for IT professionals and high-performance students.

## Technology Stack
- **React 18** with TypeScript
- **Tailwind CSS** for aesthetic UI/UX
- **Google Gemini 3 Pro** for deep reasoning and synthesis
- **Lucide Icons** for expert branding
- **React Markdown** for structured medical report rendering

## How to Use
1. Fill in the "Audit Profil Pasien" with your details.
2. Click "Konsultasikan dengan Dewan Pakar".
3. Wait for the quartet to synchronize their analysis.
4. Review your custom recovery blueprint, complete with lifestyle audits and emergency protocols.
